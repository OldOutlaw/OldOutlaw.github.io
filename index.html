About Elena

With over 8 years of experience in wedding videography, I believe every love story is unique and deserves to be told with passion, creativity, and authenticity.

My journey began when I filmed my best friend's wedding and realized the power of capturing those fleeting, precious moments that make each celebration special. Since then, I've had the honor of documenting over 200 weddings, each one teaching me something new about love, joy, and human connection.

My approach is unobtrusive yet comprehensive, ensuring I capture both the grand moments and intimate details that make your day uniquely yours. I use state-of-the-art equipment and cinematic techniques to create films that feel like a beautiful dream you can relive forever.
